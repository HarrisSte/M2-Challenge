About Me

My name is Stephanie Harris and I am currently an 8th-grade teacher at Durant Road Middle School. I have taught middle school for the last ten years and I have a passion for both teaching and learning; which is why I am persuing a life-long passion to learn how to code.

I grew up in Central New York just 40 minutes north of Syracuse. I graduated from the State University of New York at Oswego with both my English and Adolescent Education degrees. From there, I decided to move south for my career and warmer weather.

My hobbies span many different things; reading, writing, hiking, traveling, and playing videogames during my free time. I also have four pets; two cats named Finn and Bee, as well as two dogs named Dogmeat and Malakai.
